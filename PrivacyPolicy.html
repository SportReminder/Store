GENERAL PROVISIONS

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") is an official document of SPORT INVESTMENT LTD, Registered Business Number 12672106, located in Bristol, UK ("Operator"). The Policy defines the process for handling personal data and outlines measures taken to ensure the security of personal data for individuals ("Data Subjects") using the services and products available on the Operator's website www.sportreminder.shop.

1. Data Handling and Security

1.1. The Policy applies to all personal data received from Data Subjects during registration on the Website.
1.2. This Policy complies with the UK’s Data Protection Act 2018 and aims to prevent breaches related to personal data handling.
1.3. The Operator prioritizes the protection of individual rights and freedoms regarding the handling of personal data.

2. Types of Personal Data Collected

2.1. The Operator may collect the following personal data: name, date of birth, phone number, email address, and delivery address.
2.2. Other non-personal information, such as IP address and browser type, may be collected automatically.

3. Purpose of Processing Personal Data

3.1. Personal data is processed to fulfill orders, provide customer service, and manage marketing campaigns, subject to the Data Subject’s consent.

4. Data Subject Consent

4.1. The Data Subject provides consent by completing registration forms or making an order. Consent can be withdrawn at any time by notifying the Operator via official communication channels.

5. Cross-Border Data Transfer

5.1. The Operator ensures that cross-border transfers of personal data are made to countries with adequate data protection.
5.2. Cross-border data transfers to countries without sufficient data protection require written consent from the Data Subject.

6. Final Provisions

6.1. The current version of the Policy is publicly available on www.sportreminder.shop. Any changes will be reflected on the website.
